2. Copyright Infringement Notification (DMCA Takedown Request)

If you are a copyright owner (or authorized to act on behalf of one) and believe that any content on File Magics violates your rights, you must send a written DMCA Notice containing the following information:

Your DMCA Notice Must Include:

1. Identification of the copyrighted work

Provide details of the copyrighted material you believe has been infringed.

2. Identification of the infringing material

Provide the exact URL(s) on File Magics where the infringing content appears.

3. Contact Information

Include your full name, email address, phone number, and address so we may contact you.

4. A statement of good faith belief

Example:
"I have a good faith belief that the use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law."

5. A statement under penalty of perjury

Example:
"I swear, under penalty of perjury, that the information in this notice is accurate and that I am the copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner."

6. Your physical or electronic signature

This can be your typed full name.

5. Counter-Notification (If You Believe Material Was Wrongly Removed)

If your content was removed and you believe it was a mistake, you may file a DMCA Counter-Notice. This must include:

  • Identification of the removed material
  • Your contact information
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the material was wrongly removed
  • Your consent to jurisdiction of Indian courts
  • Your electronic or physical signature

Once received, we may restore the content unless the original complainant files a legal action within 14 business days.
