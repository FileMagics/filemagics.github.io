Welcome to File Magics

File Magics is a comprehensive platform offering 50+ browser-based text processing tools designed to simplify your digital workflow. Founded in 2023 by Raunak Anand, our mission is to provide fast, efficient, and completely private text manipulation tools that work entirely in your browser.

Unlike traditional online tools that upload your data to servers, every File Magics tool processes your text locally on your device. This means your sensitive information never leaves your computer, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Our Privacy Commitment

At File Magics, we take privacy seriously. Here's what sets us apart:

  • No Data Storage: We don't store any text you process through our tools. Once you close the tab, your data is gone forever.
  • No Tracking: We don't track your usage across tools or sessions. Your activity remains private.
  • No Account Required: Use all our tools without creating an account or providing any personal information.
  • Local Processing: All text processing happens in your browser using JavaScript. Your data never leaves your device.
  • Transparent Operations: We're open about how our tools work and what happens to your data (nothing!).

This approach aligns with global privacy standards including GDPR, CCPA, and India's Data Protection Principles.
